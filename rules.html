The Bay Open 2015 - On The Day

Listen Up

Rules are there for a reason. Pay attention.

  1. Membership must be valid on the 11th of January. Memberships will be checked against the BFA database. Membership WILL NOT be available on the day.
  2. Fencers must ensure that their kit complies with BFA safety standards.
  3. Electric foil bibs are required for this event.
  4. Fencers must be at least 13 years of age before the 1st of January 2015.
  5. The competition will be run under the governance laid out in Rules for Competitions.
  6. No consent form = No fence
    1. Over 18s can complete a consent form at the check-in desk or bring a completed consent form with them.
    2. Under 18s must bring a completed consent form or have one completed by a parent or legal guardian on the day of the competition.
  7. Everybody be cool. You, be cool.
